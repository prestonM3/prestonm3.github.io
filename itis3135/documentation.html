Overview

The Passive Mouse theme is a gentle, cozy aesthetic centered around a quiet mouse who enjoys reading, collecting small items, and living in a peaceful nook. This theme is used across the site to create a consistent tone and visual identity.

It emphasizes warmth, calmness, and simplicity — reflecting the personality of Passive Mouse.

Mascot Design

Passive Mouse is designed as a soft, introverted character who prefers quiet activities. The mascot is intentionally non‑aggressive and expressive through subtle gestures rather than loud actions.

  • Soft, rounded shapes
  • Neutral or warm color palette
  • Calm facial expressions
  • Small scale to emphasize coziness

Cozy Reading Nook

The Cozy Reading Nook is the central environment for Passive Mouse. It represents a peaceful space filled with miniature books, journals, bookmarks, and soft lighting.

This environment supports the theme by reinforcing comfort, warmth, and quiet creativity.

  • Miniature books and journals
  • Bookmarks and reading tools
  • Soft lanterns and warm lighting
  • Small decorative items

Inventory System

The inventory page lists items found in Passive Mouse’s reading nook. Each item includes a category, condition, availability, and a cozy rating.

This system helps organize the theme’s objects in a structured, readable format.

  • Item Name
  • Category (Book, Accessory, Decor, Tool)
  • Condition (New, Used, Worn)
  • Availability (In Stock, Limited, Out of Stock)
  • Cozy Rating (1–3)

Usage

The Passive Mouse theme is applied across multiple pages, including:

  • The Inventory Page
  • The Documentation Page
  • The Playing Cards Page
  • The Product Page

Each page uses elements of the theme to maintain consistency and reinforce the cozy, passive aesthetic.
